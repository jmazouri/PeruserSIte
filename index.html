Internet Galleries

Import galleries from your favorite image sites like Imgur and 4Chan, and browse through them with ease.

DirectShow Integration

By using your system's preinstalled DirectShow filters, Peruser can support any media type you can find a codec for. That includes WebM, MKV, MP4, GIF, and a multitude of other image types. The possibilities are endless!

Easly Extensible

Integrated plugin support allows you to download a number of third-party library parsers for your favorite obscure gallery site.
